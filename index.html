About me

I'm a Biomedical Engineering graduate student at the University of Technology Sydney, passionate about creating technology that makes healthcare accessible, equitable, and human-centred.

My work spans IoT health monitoring, machine learning diagnostics, and assistive devices — always grounded in empathy for the people who will use them. I believe the best engineering is invisible: it just quietly makes life better.

I'm passionate about the intersection of healthcare and design, drawn to crafting intuitive, accessible product experiences — and actively seeking opportunities to grow as a Product Designer within the medical and tech space.

Away from coursework, you'll find me sketching, tending to my garden, cooking, or singing — activities that, much like engineering, require creativity, patience, and attention to detail. Since moving to Australia, I've also become a fan of coastal walks, finding inspiration in the outdoors.
